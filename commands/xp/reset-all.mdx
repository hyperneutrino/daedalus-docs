---
title: "/xp reset-all"
---

<Note>By default, this command requires the **Administrator** permission.</Note>

Reset the XP of everyone in the server. This will set their XP back to 0 (including their monthly, weekly, and daily XP gains). Their roles will be updated
according to your reward role configuration (including restoring roles that are removed at certain levels). You will be prompted to confirm this action.

## Syntax

```
/xp reset-all
```
